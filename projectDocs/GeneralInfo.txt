--- Chipotle Chooser: General Info ---

	Name: Chipotle Chooser

	About: Chipotle Chooser is an online application which helps users pick a meal from the restaurant based on their personal preferences combined with nutritional needs and goals. The application is built to help customers easily make healthy choices when visiting Chipotle franches.
